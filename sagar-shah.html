About Sagar Shah

Sagar has been helping companies translate their business ideas into financial forecasts. These financial forecasts have ultimately helped clients raise capital (debt/equity), prepare business valuations, make acquisitions, develop their budgeting and forecasting, and identify the most efficient deployment of capital.

He has advised diverse businesses and helps companies and their Directors make relevant, informed and purposeful decisions with insight. His experience spans companies of different sizes and maturity within the agriculture, real estate, manufacturing (FMCGs), finance, hospitality and fintech sectors.

As a financial consultant, Sagar has performed several financial due diligence projects as well as forensic audit assignments.

As a former auditor, he has strong technical accounting knowledge and has signed off on audits for over 400 different clients in sectors including, but not limited to: paper & plastic manufacturing, real estate, insurance, banking, hospitals, hotels, schools, agriculture as well as providing advisory services on strengthening financial and internal controls. With this deep understanding of how businesses work, he has helped streamline internal operations of a number of our clients.

He advises clients in Asia, East Africa, Australia, Europe & the USA.

Sagar is a CPA and holds a Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics, Operational Research, Statistics & Economics & a Post Graduate Diploma in Actuarial Science.
